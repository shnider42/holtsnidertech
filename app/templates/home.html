Approach

Grounded, direct, and built around the actual problem.

How the work is approached

  • Start with the real issue, not just the software name attached to it.
  • Understand the business context before recommending tools.
  • Favor clarity, maintainability, and usefulness over hype.
  • Reduce complexity where possible instead of adding to it.
  • Keep communication understandable and direct.

Who this is for

  • Small businesses that need strong technical judgment without a full internal team.
  • Operators dealing with aging systems, messy workflows, or unclear tooling.
  • Clients who need help evaluating websites, automation, vendors, or software setups.
  • People who want someone who can troubleshoot and explain at the same time.

About

Holtsnider Tech is built around practical judgment.

A lot of businesses do not need more buzzwords, more platforms, or more technical theater. They need someone who can look at a situation clearly, figure out where the friction actually is, and help make the system more workable.

Holtsnider Tech is built for that kind of work. It sits at the intersection of troubleshooting, process improvement, technical translation, and common sense. Sometimes that means cleaning up data. Sometimes it means improving a workflow. Sometimes it means helping a client understand what a tool, platform, or project is really asking of them.

The goal is clarity, momentum, and solutions that make sense in the real world.
